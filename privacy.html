Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Octavia by LunarTech ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our AI dubbing and translation platform.

2. Data Processing & Voice Privacy

Your uploaded audio and video files, along with any generated voice models, are strictly your property. We do not use your content to train global multi-tenant models without your explicit written consent. All voice cloning and dubbing operations are performed in isolated processing environments with enterprise-grade security.

3. Data Collection

We may collect certain information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and payment information when you register for an account.
  • Media Files: Audio and video files you upload for dubbing, translation, or voice cloning purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the platform, such as feature usage and session duration, to help us improve the product.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Processed high-resolution video files and audio assets are stored securely in encrypted cloud storage. We retain your media files for 30 days after processing completion, after which they are permanently deleted unless you have an active Enterprise plan with custom retention policies.

5. Third-Party AI Services

Octavia uses third-party AI translation and speech synthesis APIs to deliver our services. We have strict data processing agreements in place with all providers to ensure your data is NOT used to train their models and is processed in compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of all data we hold about you or opt out of certain data collection practices. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@lunartech.ai
